Engineering & Equipment Pte. Ltd.

Zonida Shematec Engineering & Equipment Pte. Ltd. is a leading metal packaging equipment manufacturer. We specialize in manufacturing can making machinery for food & beverage can, aerosol can, DRD can, chemical general can, lock Seamed can and can lids, such as: Slitter, CNC Press, Welder, Coating Machine, Induction Oven, Combiner, Palletizer/De-palletizer, Cartoning Machine, Strapper & Wrapper, etc.


With decades of experience and research in assembling Can-Making Line, End-Making Line and Filling Line, we can provide one-stop premier turnkey solutions at your requirements.


Since established, we have been conducting the business principle of responsibility, efficiency and client first. Focusing on packing innovation, we have provided superior service for numbers of leading companies in can making industry worldwide. Our professions and efforts earned good reputation and trust from customers.


Our company was founded in Singapore, and the factory is located in Qingdao, Shandong Province, China. It’s 20 kilometers away from Qingdao Port (CNQIN), 40-minute journey to Qingdao International Airport. Sincerely welcome you to visit and cooperate with us.
